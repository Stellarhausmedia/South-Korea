Welcome, international patients

Dentistry that speaks your language.

Moving cities — or just visiting — shouldn't mean leaving your smile to chance. At Yonsei JW Dental, an English-speaking dentist sits with you to explain your options clearly, answer every question, and plan treatment at a pace that feels right.

From your first message to your final review appointment, you're cared for in plain English — with Japanese and Chinese support on hand, too. No translation apps, no guesswork. Just a clear, unhurried conversation about your teeth.

정원 · The garden

A place to bring your smile into order.

In Korean, 정원 means garden — and that's exactly the feeling we set out to create. Set in Daechi, one of Seoul's most considered, education-focused neighbourhoods, our clinic is a quiet retreat from the pace of the city.

Like tending a garden, aligning a smile is patient, deliberate work. We believe the experience should feel that way too: calm, scholarly, and reassuringly clear — every stage understood before it begins.
