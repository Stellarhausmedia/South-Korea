The name & the place

Rois means kings — and you'll be treated like one.

In French, rois means kings. It's a high bar to set, and one our team takes to heart: care that feels considered, unhurried and genuinely yours.

We're part of a respected dental group, which means established methods and dependable standards — delivered with the personal attention of a neighbourhood clinic. And our neighbourhood happens to be Myeongdong: the bright, buzzing heart of Seoul that visitors from around the world pass through every day.

For an expat settling in or a traveller passing through, that's a rare combination — serious dentistry, a familiar language, and a location you can find without a second thought.
