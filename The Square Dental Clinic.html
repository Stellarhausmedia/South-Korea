03 — The Clinic

Built on the idea that good care should feel composed, not complicated.

The name says it plainly. A square is steady, balanced, dependable — and that's the kind of dentistry we set out to practise from our home in D Tower, beside historic Gwanghwamun. A mid-size clinic with the calm of a boutique and the breadth of a full-service practice.

Our team is genuinely set up for international and English-speaking patients. One of our doctors trained in California, and that perspective shapes how we talk things through: plainly, patiently, and always with your understanding first. You'll never feel rushed past a question.

The result is care that feels structured and unhurried — measured planning, honest options, and a finish you can be proud of. Exactly what you'd hope to find, right in the civic heart of Seoul.
