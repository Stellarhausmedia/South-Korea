03Our story

A quarter-century, one chair at a time

U&I has practised in the same corner of Apgujeong for twenty-five years — long enough to treat families across generations, and to keep refining a single idea: dentistry is personal first, technical second.

  1. 2000

    The doors open in Apgujeong

    A boutique orthodontic practice takes root in Sinsa-dong, Gangnam — small by design, so every patient is known by name.

  2. ···

    Research meets the chair

    Orthodontic work earns a place in American journals — and that scholarship comes straight back to how each case is planned.

  3. Now

    A clinic that speaks English

    Today U&I welcomes expats and visitors in fluent English, pairing that same scholarly precision with warm, unhurried care.
