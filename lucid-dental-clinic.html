A Clinic That Speaks Your Language

You'll never feel lost in translation here.

Moving to Seoul is enough to navigate without decoding a dental chart in Korean. At LUCID, your consultation, your treatment plan, and every follow-up happen in clear, confident English.

Our team treats a truly international community day to day — expats, students and visitors, with care also offered in Japanese and Chinese. You ask the questions you'd ask back home, and you get real answers before anything begins.

  • English from hello to follow-upConsults, quotes and aftercare explained plainly — no guesswork, no awkward translation apps.

Our Studio
